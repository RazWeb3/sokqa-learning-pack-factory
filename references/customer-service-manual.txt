Customer Service Manual (Convenience Store)

Greeting
- Smile and greet the customer clearly.
- Use polite phrases such as: いらっしゃいませ / ありがとうございます / 少々お待ちください / 申し訳ございません

Register Handling
- Repeat the total amount and confirm before payment.
- If there is any uncertainty, ask a senior staff member immediately.

Problem Handling
- Stay calm and listen to the customer.
- Apologize politely and offer the next step clearly.
